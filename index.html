Who We Are

About Us

Sea Beach Services & Traders is a Chittagong-based logistics company specialising in ship chartering, inland cargo transportation, and commission agency services. Founded in January 1999, the company has spent over two decades facilitating the seamless movement of goods from port to destination across Bangladesh.

Operating as a trusted single-owner enterprise, Sea Beach Services & Traders brings hands-on expertise to every operation — from negotiating vessel charters and coordinating with port authorities, to managing inland distribution networks that keep trade moving efficiently within the country.

Built on a foundation of reliability, transparency, and long-standing client relationships, the company plays an active role in supporting Bangladesh's import and trade logistics infrastructure out of Chittagong — the country's primary port city.

Proudly serving Bangladesh's trade and maritime sector from the port city of Chittagong since 1999.

What We Do

Our Services

Ship Chartering

We facilitate the chartering of vessels for cargo transportation, connecting clients with the right ships for their specific trade requirements. With deep knowledge of the regional maritime market, we negotiate competitive charter terms and manage all documentation and coordination from contract to port arrival. Whether for bulk cargo, general freight, or specialised shipments, we ensure every charter is handled with precision and care.

Inland Carrier Services

Once cargo arrives at port, we manage the full inland distribution chain — transporting goods from Chittagong port to destinations across Bangladesh efficiently and on schedule. Our established network of carriers and logistics partners ensures goods move swiftly from ship to shore and onward to their final destination, no matter where in the country. We handle all coordination, documentation, and delivery oversight so clients can focus on their business.

Commission Agent Services

We act as a trusted commission agent, representing buyers and sellers in cargo and trade transactions across the region. Leveraging over two decades of industry relationships and market knowledge, we facilitate deals, negotiate terms, and ensure all parties are well-represented throughout the transaction. Our hands-on, client-first approach means every deal is handled with the same care as our own.
